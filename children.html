Child-Safe Islamic Support

Islam strictly forbids harm to children. Children are an amanah (trust) from Allah.

لَيْسَ مِنَّا مَنْ لَمْ يَرْحَمْ صَغِيرَنَا

“He is not from us who does not show mercy to our young.”

If a child is in danger, contact local emergency services immediately.
